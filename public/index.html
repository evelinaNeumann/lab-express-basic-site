About TikTok CEO

The CEO of TikTok is Zhang Yiming. He is a Chinese internet entrepreneur and software engineer who co-founded ByteDance, the parent company of TikTok, in 2012. Zhang Yiming was born on April 1, 1983, in Longyan, Fujian province, China.

Before founding ByteDance, Zhang Yiming worked as a software engineer at Microsoft and then at the Chinese startup Kuxun, where he developed a travel recommendation algorithm. In 2012, he founded ByteDance and launched the news aggregator app Toutiao, which uses machine learning algorithms to personalize news feeds for individual users.

Under Zhang Yiming's leadership, ByteDance has grown rapidly and become one of the world's most valuable startups, with a valuation of over $400 billion as of 2021. TikTok, which was launched in 2016, has become one of the most popular social media platforms in the world, with over a billion active users.

TikTok CEO's Works

Zhang Yiming, the CEO of TikTok's parent company ByteDance, is responsible for leading the development of TikTok, one of the world's most popular social media platforms. TikTok is a video-sharing app that allows users to create and share short, 15-second videos set to music or other audio clips. The app has become particularly popular among younger audiences, and has been downloaded more than 2 billion times worldwide since its launch in 2016.
